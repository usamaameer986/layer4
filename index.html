What is Delta-E? — The number that replaces "it looks a bit off"
Delta-E is the objective distance between two colours. Lower is more accurate. Industry standard for packaging is Delta-E ≤ 2.0.
Delta-E scale — colour difference meter
1.4 ✓
2.0
0–2.0 PASS
2–4 Borderline
4+ FAIL — reprint
What different Delta-E values feel like to a human eye:
